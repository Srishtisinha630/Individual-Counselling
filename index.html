About Us

At Individual Counseling, we are dedicated to providing personalized mental health services to help you achieve a better quality of life. Our experienced counselors are here to support you through every step of your journey.

Our mission is to offer compassionate and effective counseling services tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. We believe in the power of therapy to transform lives and are committed to fostering a safe and supportive environment for all our clients.

Our Services

One-on-One Counseling

Personalized sessions to address your specific needs. Our experienced counselors work with you to develop strategies and tools to improve your mental health and well-being. Each session is tailored to help you achieve your personal goals and overcome challenges.

  • Address personal issues and challenges
  • Develop coping strategies
  • Improve mental health and well-being
  • Build confidence and self-esteem

Price: ₹800 per hour

Couples Counseling

Helping couples navigate relationship challenges. We provide a safe space for partners to communicate openly, resolve conflicts, and strengthen their bond. Our counselors use effective techniques to improve understanding and foster a deeper connection between partners.

  • Enhance communication skills
  • Resolve conflicts and misunderstandings
  • Strengthen emotional connection
  • Develop mutual respect and trust

Price: ₹1000 per hour

Family Therapy

Supporting families in building healthy dynamics. Our family therapy sessions focus on improving communication, resolving conflicts, and fostering a supportive environment for all family members. We aim to strengthen family bonds and promote mutual understanding.

  • Improve family communication
  • Resolve family conflicts
  • Build a supportive family environment
  • Promote mutual understanding and respect

Price: ₹900 per hour

Online Counseling

Convenient sessions from the comfort of your home. Our online counseling services offer flexibility and accessibility, allowing you to receive the support you need from anywhere. We use secure and confidential platforms to ensure your privacy and comfort during sessions.

  • Accessible from anywhere
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Confidential and secure sessions
  • Support for a wide range of issues

Price: ₹700 per hour
